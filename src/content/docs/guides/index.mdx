## What is KSP2 Redux?

KSP2 Redux is a free, community-made mod for Kerbal Space Program 2 that aims to implement most of the features promised by the original developers. We focus on:

- **Performance Improvements:** 30-56% better FPS across different scenarios
- **Bug Fixes:** Hundreds of bugs fixed, from physics glitches to UI errors
- **Promised Features:** Colonies, interstellar travel, multiplayer, and more
- **Modding Support:** Revolutionary Unity Editor integration for mod development

Redux is developed by a team of passionate volunteer KSP2 modders who want to see the game reach its full potential.

## Current Status

We are currently in **Stage 0: Foundation** beta testing. This stage focuses on:

- Performance optimizations (up to 56% FPS improvements)
- Bug fixes and stability improvements
- Debug tools for players and modders
- Enhanced modding SDK with Unity Editor integration
- Vessel ISRU and heat management systems
- Tech tree improvements

The next stages will bring colonies, full resource management, interstellar travel, and multiplayer!

## Important Notes

- **Beta Software:** Redux is currently in beta. Some features may be incomplete or buggy.
- **No Official Affiliation:** Redux has no connection to the former KSP2 developers or Private Division.
- **Volunteer Project:** Redux is developed entirely by volunteers in their free time.
- **Separate Installation:** Redux installs separately from vanilla KSP2 - your original game remains untouched.

## Support

Need help or found a bug?

- **Discord:** Ask questions in **#🟡redux-chat** or report bugs in **#🟡redux-bugs**
- **FAQ:** Check our [Frequently Asked Questions](/faq/) page

We appreciate your patience and feedback as we continue to improve Redux!
